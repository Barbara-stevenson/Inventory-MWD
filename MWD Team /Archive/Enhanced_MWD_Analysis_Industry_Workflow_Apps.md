### Why This Matters

- **Manufacturing workflows (43.4%)** NEED Items app for BOM and inventory tracking

## 5. Strategic Recommendations by Segment

### For Industrial Equipment & Machinery (37 companies)

**Current State:**
- High Sales Orders usage (manufacturing workflow)
- Low Items app adoption
- Very High product complexity

**Recommendations:**
1. Drive Items app adoption for BOM management
2. Integrate Work Orders with inventory consumption
3. Enable serial number tracking for high-value equipment

### For Construction & Building Materials (25 companies)

**Current State:**
- Balanced Estimates and Sales Orders usage
- Mixed workflow types (manufacturing + distribution)
- Project-based operations

**Recommendations:**
1. Project-based inventory allocation
2. Multi-warehouse visibility (regional distribution centers)
3. Job costing tied to material consumption

### For Medical & Healthcare (17 companies)

**Current State:**
- Highest regulatory compliance needs
- FDA-regulated manufacturing
- Very High product complexity

**Recommendations:**
1. Lot/batch tracking capabilities
2. Expiration date management
3. Audit trail for regulatory compliance
4. Serial number tracking for medical devices
