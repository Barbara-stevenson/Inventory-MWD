# MWD Industry Actual Workflows Analysis
**Document Date:** 2026-01-12
**Analysis Focus:** What companies ACTUALLY do in Method CRM based on engagement depth

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis reveals the **actual workflows** MWD companies perform in Method CRM, based on:
- **Daily page load volume** (how much they use each app)
- **Adoption rates** (what % of companies use each app)
- **Engagement scores** (volume × adoption = true engagement)

### Key Finding: The Engagement Gap

While all MWD industries follow similar "ideal" CRM workflows on paper, **actual usage reveals critical gaps**:

🔴 **Inventory management apps severely underutilized** despite manufacturing/distribution needs
🔴 **Production tracking (Work Orders) nearly absent** despite 60%+ being manufacturers
🟡 **Front-office activities (quotes, communication) dominate** back-office operations neglected
🟢 **Lead-to-cash core is strong** but incomplete workflow execution

---

## Methodology: Engagement-Based Workflow Analysis

**Traditional approach:** Logical business process order (Contacts → Quote → Order → Invoice)
**This approach:** Rank apps by actual engagement depth

### Engagement Score Formula
```
Engagement Score = Daily Page Loads × (Adoption Rate / 100)
```

This reveals what companies REALLY spend time doing, weighted by how many actually use it.

### Usage Level Classifications
- **Heavy Use:** >1000 daily page loads
- **Moderate Use:** 100-1000 daily page loads  
- **Light Use:** 10-100 daily page loads
- **Minimal Use:** <10 daily page loads

### Adoption Level Classifications
- **Universal:** >80% of accounts
- **Majority:** 50-80% of accounts
- **Common:** 25-50% of accounts
- **Niche:** <25% of accounts
