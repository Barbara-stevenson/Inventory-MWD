Key Observations

  • Mobile-first picking: Picking tasks are sent to the Warehouse App on mobile devices
  • Status-driven flow: Pack some → Ready for packing → Picked → Packed → Delivered
  • Batch tracking: Two options - pre-assign batches or let operators choose during picking
  • Partial fulfillment: "Pack some" creates separate picking tasks with SO suffixes (e.g., SO-001 (1/2))
  • Recovery: Revert button allows undoing packed status back to "Not delivered"
