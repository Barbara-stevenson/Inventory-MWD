## What This Section Covers

Competitive intelligence for winning MWD deals. Every card is built from real competitive data, actual customer feedback, and honest assessment of where Method wins and where it doesn't.

**Three rules for competitive conversations:**

1. **Lead with Method's value, not competitor weaknesses.** Tear-down selling doesn't work with risk-averse MWD buyers.
2. **Be honest about gaps.** These buyers have done their homework. Getting caught overpromising costs more than the deal.
3. **Match the competitor to the conversation.** Different competitors show up at different deal stages and company sizes.
