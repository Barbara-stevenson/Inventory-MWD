# Method vs. NetSuite — MWD Battlecard
### Card 10a | Section 3: Competitive Intelligence

**Audience:** Sales reps, presales
**When to use:** Prospect mentions NetSuite, has been quoted by NetSuite, or says "we're looking at ERP"

**Agent:** Dex — Battlecard Builder | Execution Squad

---

## The Numbers

| Dimension | Method | NetSuite |
|---|---|---|
| Per-user pricing | $27-$73/user/month | $99/user/month (base) |
| Platform/setup fee | None | $25K-$75K mandatory |
| Total year 1 (5 users) | ~$2,700-$4,380 | $30,940-$80,940+ |
| Implementation | 1-2 months | 6-12 months |
| QB sync | Native two-way | Third-party only ($25K-$150K setup) |
| Customization | DIY + DEP ($325/mo) | Developer-heavy ($150-$300/hr) |
| Contract | Month-to-month | Annual (typically multi-year) |

---

## When Method Wins / Loses

**Wins:** Prospect keeping QB. Prospect got quoted and walked away on price. Need operational CRM, not full ERP. Value speed (1-2 mo vs. 6-12). Want a partner, not a project.

**Loses:** Prospect needs full MRP (BOM, kitting, lot tracking, shop floor). Has decided to leave QB. Has enterprise budget and enterprise needs ($30M+, 100+ users). Needs native e-commerce.

---

## The One Question

**"Are you planning to stay on QuickBooks for accounting?"** If yes — NetSuite wants them off QB. That changes everything.

## Appendix: Talk Track & Objection Handling

### Talk Track

**Opening:** "NetSuite is a serious platform. The real question is whether you need everything it does, or the parts that matter at a price that makes sense."

**If keeping QB:** "NetSuite wants you off QuickBooks — their whole model is replacing your accounting. Method extends QB, not replaces it."

**Cost:** "A typical Method team of 5-8 users runs $2,700-$7,000/year. Add DEP and you're under $11K. NetSuite's first year is typically $30K-$80K before customization."

**Speed:** "NetSuite: 6-12 months. Method: 1-2 months."

**Customer proof:** Vintage Makers rejected NetSuite as "too heavy and expensive." Brown Safe has 177 custom screens at a fraction of enterprise cost. One customer spent nearly 10 years and over $100K with NetSuite before switching.

### Objection Handling

| They Say | You Say |
|---|---|
| "NetSuite does everything in one place" | "It does — that's why it costs $25K+ setup and takes 6-12 months. Do you need everything it does?" |
| "Our consultant recommended NetSuite" | "Consultants earn implementation fees. What specific problems are you solving?" |
| "We'll outgrow Method" | "Our customers at $25M with 54 locations haven't. 159-177 custom screens." |
| "NetSuite has better reporting" | "For finance, yes. For operational KPIs, Method's custom dashboards are built around your metrics." |

### Landmines
- Don't say Method replaces NetSuite — different tools for different needs
- Don't promise inventory management — follow inventory messaging exactly
- Don't compare features alone — compare on fit
