## The MWD Positioning in One Paragraph

Method is a Customizable CRM built for manufacturers and distributors who run on QuickBooks. These businesses have outgrown spreadsheets but aren't ready for enterprise ERP. Method gives them operational control — quoting, order management, inventory visibility, mobile field access — without ripping out the accounting system they depend on. Native two-way QuickBooks sync. Real customization, not just custom fields. Expert support that treats them like a partner, not a ticket number. Starting at $27/user/month.

---

## Five Things to Always Get Right

1. **It's "Customizable CRM" — not "custom CRM" or "customized CRM."** Customizable means it adapts. That's the category.
2. **QuickBooks sync is native and two-way.** This is Method's biggest differentiator. Every competitor either can't sync with QB or needs a third-party connector.
3. **Inventory is handled through customization, not a native module.** Method doesn't have built-in inventory management. But it's customizable enough that customers build inventory solutions inside it — multi-warehouse tracking, serial numbers, bin locations, back orders. Some integrate third-party tools like SOS Inventory via API. Lead with the customization story, not a feature claim.
4. **The campaign line is "Yes, There's a Method for That."** The four Yes pillars: Yes to real-world business, Yes to quote-to-cash, Yes to QuickBooks, Yes to expert support.
5. **The real competitor is inertia.** Most MWD businesses aren't comparing CRMs. They're deciding whether to change at all. 71% of prospects run QB only, another 15% run QB + spreadsheets. Enterprise CRMs combined are under 7% of the competitive set. "QuickBooks + spreadsheets" is the status quo we're up against.
