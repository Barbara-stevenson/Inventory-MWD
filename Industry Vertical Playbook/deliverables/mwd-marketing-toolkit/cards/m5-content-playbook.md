## Campaign Ideas

### "Yes, There's a Method for That" — MWD Edition
Run the campaign line across channels with MWD-specific use cases:
- "Need to track 3,400 SKUs across 6 states? Yes, there's a Method for that."
- "Need one system for quoting, invoicing, and production tracking? Yes, there's a Method for that."
- "Need your field reps to see customer history on the road? Yes, there's a Method for that."
- "Need to track inventory your way — without leaving QuickBooks? Yes, there's a Method for that."

### "Keep QuickBooks. Get Everything Else." Campaign
Position Method as the operational extension of QuickBooks:
- Landing page: "What QuickBooks Can't Do for Manufacturers (and What Method Can)"
- Blog series: 5 posts, each covering one operational gap QB has and how Method fills it
- Email sequence: One gap per email, customer proof for each

### Customer Spotlight Series
One customer story per month. Structure: their business, their problem, what they built in Method, the result. Keep it short (500 words max per post). Video versions if customers agree.

## Trigger-Based Outreach Angles

The highest-converting play for MWD is trigger-based outreach — reaching prospects when a specific event forces them to reevaluate their systems. See [M6 Sales Quick-Reference](m6-sales-quick-reference.md) for the five buying triggers. Here's a starting email angle for each, with prospect frequency from transcript data [1]:

| Trigger (from M6) | Frequency [1] | Email angle | Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| Growth / scaling | 9% | "One CRM for every location" | "You just added a location. Are your systems keeping up, or are you about to double your spreadsheets too?" |
| System frustration | 7% | "There's a better way" | "QuickBooks is just, like, it's so delayed, I don't know what the hell's going on." Sound familiar? |
| Business event (new role, acquisition) | 6% | "New chapter, clean systems" | "New leadership is a chance to stop doing things the hard way." |
| Switching from competitor | 4% | "What left with them?" | "When the person who built your process leaves, the process shouldn't leave with them." |
| QB Desktop sunset concerns | — | "Keep QuickBooks. Add the rest." | "The QB Desktop timeline is uncertain. But your operations don't have to wait." |

For persona-level targeting of these topics, see **[M2: Audience & Personas](m2-audience-personas.md)** in this toolkit.
