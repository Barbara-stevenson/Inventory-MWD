## Why This Merged

Allan's feedback: when you need proof, you want one place — not two. Customer stories and prospect voice data serve the same purpose (backing up your message), so they belong together. M3 now has:

- **Proof by use case** — customization, QB sync, quote-to-cash, multi-location, mobile, DEP, enterprise alt, inventory
- **Quotes** — customer-approved (external-safe) and prospect voice (internal-only), side by side
- **Grab & Go lines** — deployable marketing copy, organized by the pain point they address
- **Pain data** — the full 465-call breakdown (frequency, triggers, feature demand, current tools)
- **Voice Vault** — raw prospect quotes for copy inspiration (internal only)
