### Inventory Visibility — "The customization story"

**Key stats:** 26% of prospects mention inventory visibility [1] · 37% request inventory management features [1] · Method does NOT have native inventory — this is a customization + integration story

**What we can say:** Customers have built inventory solutions inside Method through customization. Multi-warehouse tracking, serial numbers, bin locations. Some integrate third-party tools like SOS Inventory via API.

**What we don't say:** That Method replaces Fishbowl, SOS, Cin7, or Katana. For deep BOM, kitting, lot tracking, or shop floor — recommend a dedicated tool + Method API integration.

**Customer implementations:**

| Customer | Approach | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Electronics company [Saelig] | API Integration | Method → SOS Inventory → QBO circular sync. View-by-warehouse button. 3x daily sync. |
| Power tools distributor [Techtronic] | Full Custom Build | Serial numbers, bin locations, multi-warehouse, back order management — all in Method. |
| Engine parts distributor [Remanufactured.com] | Clean Separation | CRM pipeline in Method, all inventory in SOS. 2-hour scheduled sync. |

**Grab & Go line (external-safe):** Inventory visibility built your way — not forced into someone else's template.

## Full Customer Snapshots

**Internal reference:** Real customer names in [brackets]. Do NOT use in external content without permission.

### Luxury Safe Manufacturer [Brown Safe]
Custom luxury safe manufacturer. 40-year-old company. 12-week production cycles. 177 custom screens. Custom Production app tracking full manufacturing cycle. QB + Avalara + Zapier. Rejected Salesforce on cost. **Best for:** Customization depth, manufacturing workflow, enterprise alternative.

### Mobility Equipment Franchise [Mobility City]
Mobility equipment franchise. 54 locations (scaling to 150-200). Sales, repairs, rentals. 159 custom screens. Compliance tracking, standardized POS, rental management. 33+ users. DEP since 2022. **Best for:** Multi-location, franchise standardization, scaling, DEP.

### Cigar & Wine Distributor [Vintage Makers]
Custom wine cellars + wholesale cigar distribution. 3,400 SKUs across 6 states. Founded 1994. Field reps on tablets. Full sales workflow. QB Desktop Enterprise. Rejected NetSuite. **Best for:** Mobile access, distribution, enterprise alternative, field operations.

### $25M Food Distributor [Europena]
$25M food distribution, transitioning into manufacturing. Two mirrored entities. CRM, reporting, iPad audits, Web to Lead. Considered switching — customization investment kept them. **Best for:** Distribution, multi-entity, mobile, customization retention.

### Medical Equipment Manufacturer [Duraline]
Manufacturing, distribution, and service. Multi-location. 15 active users across all departments. Custom apps for Equipment, Commission, Purchase History, Email Status. Active DEP. **Best for:** DEP, cross-department adoption, manufacturing + service hybrid.
