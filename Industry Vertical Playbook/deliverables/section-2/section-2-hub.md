## What This Section Covers

Section 2 answers three questions that shape every customer-facing asset:

1. **How does Method position itself for MWD buyers?** The positioning narrative and message house framework that all downstream copy ladders up to.
2. **What do we say to each persona?** Tailored messaging for Operations Managers, Owners/GMs, and Office Managers — connected to the jobs and pains identified in Section 1.
3. **What does this look like in the real world?** Industry page copy ready for the website and customer success stories built from interview evidence.

## Key Principles Across Section 2

**Messaging ladders up, not sideways.** Every claim in persona messaging, industry page copy, and customer stories traces back to the positioning narrative and message house. If a message doesn't connect to a pillar, it doesn't belong.

**Inventory honesty is non-negotiable.** Method does NOT have native inventory management. The inventory story is QB sync + customization, with two proven paths for complex needs. Card 05c exists specifically because getting this wrong costs trust with MWD buyers. See the [Inventory Override](../../reference/INVENTORY-OVERRIDE.md) for the source of truth.

**Research-backed, not template-driven.** Every proof point and customer quote connects to actual interview evidence from Section 1. No placeholder examples, no hypothetical outcomes.

**Method tone throughout.** Direct and conversational. Grade 8 vocabulary. Bold, with a touch of cheekiness — never silly. No buzzwords (streamline, drive efficiency, seamless, accelerate). Vera reviewed everything for tone before it shipped.
