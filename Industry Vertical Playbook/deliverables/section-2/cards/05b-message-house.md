## Roof: The Value Proposition

**Full version:** Method is the Customizable CRM that gives MWD businesses the operational control they've outgrown spreadsheets to get вҖ” without replacing QuickBooks or paying for enterprise ERP.

**Short version:** The operational CRM for QuickBooks-based manufacturers and distributors.

**One-liner:** Your business works a certain way. Method adapts to match it.

**Campaign anchor:** Yes, There's a Method for That.

---

## Pillar 1: Yes to Real World Business

**Pillar promise:** Build the system that matches how your business actually works вҖ” not how software companies think you should work.

**MWD translation:** Your 12-week production cycle, your multi-location operations, your specific quoting rules, your custom equipment tracking вҖ” Method bends to fit your business. Not the other way around.

### Proof Points

### Key Messages

- "We don't give you a template and say 'make it work.' We give you the tools to build exactly what you need."

## Message House Usage Rules

1. **Every piece of MWD copy should connect to a pillar.** If it doesn't ladder up, it doesn't belong.
2. **The roof is the test.** Can you trace the claim back to "operational control without replacing QB or paying enterprise prices"? If not, rewrite.
3. **Proof points are mandatory.** No pillar claim without customer evidence or market data behind it.
4. **Tone check:** Direct. Conversational. Grade 8 vocabulary. No buzzwords. Would a real person say this to another real person? If not, rewrite.
5. **Inventory claims must follow Card 05c.** The message house does NOT claim native inventory management.
