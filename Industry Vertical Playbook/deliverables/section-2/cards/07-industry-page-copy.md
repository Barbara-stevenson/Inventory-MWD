## FAQ Section

**Q: Does Method replace QuickBooks?**
No. Method extends QuickBooks. Your accounting stays in QB. Method adds the operational layer — CRM, workflows, custom apps, dashboards. Two-way sync keeps everything connected.

**Q: Does Method handle inventory?**
Method works with your QuickBooks inventory data — stock levels, items, and availability are accessible inside Method. For complex inventory needs (BOM, lot tracking, multi-warehouse management), we support third-party inventory tool integrations and custom builds inside Method. Both approaches are used by real customers.

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
Most MWD businesses are up and running in 1-2 months. Complex implementations with heavy customization may take longer — that's where DEP helps.

**Q: What if I need something custom?**
That's where Method is different. Custom screens, custom fields, custom automations — built around your workflow, not a template. Our Dedicated Enhancement Plan ($325/month) gives you a dedicated expert who builds it with you.

## Page Copy Guidelines

1. **Tone:** Direct, conversational, grade 8 vocabulary. No buzzwords. Would a real person say this to another real person?
2. **Proof:** Every section connects to real customer evidence. No hypothetical examples.
3. **Inventory honesty:** The FAQ answer follows Card 05c guidelines exactly. Don't expand beyond what's there.
4. **Campaign line:** "Yes, There's a Method for That" appears once in the solution section header. Don't overuse it.
5. **QB versions:** The hero supporting line mentions both Desktop and Online. Don't favor one over the other.
6. **Pricing:** Use the figures in this card. They match the single source of truth. Don't round or estimate.
