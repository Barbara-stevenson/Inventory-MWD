# MWD Market Overview

**Audience:** Sales leadership, marketing, new reps onboarding

---

## Why MWD, Why Now

Manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution is Method's highest-priority vertical. These businesses share a profile: they run on QuickBooks, they've outgrown spreadsheets, and they need operational control QB alone can't provide — but they're not ready to rip out QB for a full ERP.

## Two Core Segments, One Common Thread

Method's top 200 MWD accounts break down as 101 manufacturing and 71 wholesale/distribution companies, with 28 retail accounts that often overlap with distribution. Many operate hybrid models — a manufacturer that also distributes, a distributor with a retail storefront.

**Manufacturing (with Distribution)**
Design, produce, and ship goods. Top sub-industries by LTV: Food & Beverage ($953K), Construction & Building ($900K), Healthcare & Medical ($772K), Industrial ($757K).
Examples: Brown Safe (luxury safes, 12-week production cycle, 177 custom screens), Duraline Systems (medical equipment — manufactures, distributes, and services), Vintage Makers (custom wine cellars + wholesale cigar distribution across 6 states).

**Wholesale & Distribution**
Receive inventory, manage allocation, sell through channels, ship. Includes food distribution, industrial supply, franchise distribution models.
Examples: Europena (food distribution, $25M revenue, two mirrored companies), Mobility City (54 franchise locations scaling to 150-200), Vintage Makers (3,400 cigar SKUs across 6 states), Conequip (construction equipment parts distribution).

**The common thread:** Both segments use QuickBooks for accounting and need an operational layer QB doesn't provide. Many companies span both — manufacturing their own products while also distributing third-party goods. The lines blur in practice, and Method serves the overlap.

## Addressable Market

QuickBooks dominates US small business accounting (62-84% market share, ~29M US businesses). Within that:

- ~235,000-255,000 small manufacturers (US Census, 2023)
- Add warehousing and distribution firms with the same QB dependency → significantly larger
- **Method's sweet spot:** QB-using MWD businesses with 10-50 employees and $1M-$250M revenue that need operational workflows QB can't provide but aren't ready for enterprise ERP. 83% of Method's MWD accounts are B2B.
