# MWD Pricing Comparison
### Card 01e | Section 1: Market & Customer Foundation

**Audience:** Sales reps (use in pricing conversations and proposals)
**When to use:** Prospect asks "how does your pricing compare?" or is evaluating alternatives

---

## Method's Pricing

| Plan | Per User/Month | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| Contact Management | $27 | CRM basics, QB sync, contact & lead management |
| CRM Pro | $45 | Full CRM with sales pipeline, estimates, invoices, opportunities |
| CRM Enterprise | $73 | Advanced automation, custom apps, API access |

**Add-ons:** Transaction packs (required for inventory), DEP at $325/month (3 hours ongoing customization), Method Pay for payment processing.

**Typical MWD deployment** (10-15 users, CRM Pro or Enterprise): **$600-$1,200/month** before DEP.

## vs. Full CRM Platforms: Can They Do MWD Operations?

| Platform | CRM Price | Has Inventory? | Has Field Service? | Has QB Sync? | MWD Cost (10 users) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Method** | $27-$73/user | QB sync + customization | Yes (native) | Native two-way | $600-$1,200 |
| **Salesforce** | $275-$475/user | No (AppExchange) | Add-on (Service Cloud) | Third-party only | $4,750-$7,000+ |
| **HubSpot** | Free-$150+/user | No | No | Third-party only | $1,500-$3,000+ (no ops) |
| **Zoho CRM** | $14-$52/user | Separate product | Separate product | Third-party only | $520-$1,570 (no QB sync) |
| **Odoo** | $24.90-$37.40/user | Yes (strong MRP) | Custom modules | Third-party only | $249-$374 + connector |

**Bottom line:** For MWD + QB, Method is the only option with native two-way QB sync that doesn't require enterprise pricing or leaving QuickBooks.

## vs. Specialized Inventory Tools: Can They Match Method's Breadth?

| Tool | Inventory Price | Has CRM? | Has Field Service? | Has QB Sync? | What You Still Need |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fishbowl** | ~$329-$449/mo | No | No | Yes (limited) | + CRM + field service + quoting |
| **Katana** | GMV-based | No | No | No native | + CRM + QB + field service |
| **MRPeasy** | ~$50-$150/mo | Basic pipeline | No | Basic | + full CRM + field service |
| **inFlow** | ~$110-$349/mo | No | No | Yes (basic) | + CRM + field service |
| **Cin7** | ~$349-$999/mo | No | No | Yes (one-way) | + CRM + field service |

Specialized tools go deeper on inventory/manufacturing but aren't CRM tools. Total cost of inventory + separate CRM + field service often exceeds Method's all-in-one cost.

## The Pricing Gap Method Fills

| Approach | Monthly Cost (10 users) | Systems | QB Integration |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Method (all-in-one)** | **$600-$1,200** | **1 (+ QB)** | **Native two-way** |
| Salesforce + Fishbowl | $5,000-$7,500 | 3+ | Third-party + limited |
| HubSpot + Cin7 + Jobber | $2,000-$4,000 | 4+ | Third-party + one-way |
| Zoho + Zoho Inventory | $520-$1,570 | 2 (same ecosystem) | Third-party connector |
| Odoo (all-in-one) | $249-$374 + connector | 1 | Third-party (or leave QB) |
| NetSuite | $5,000-$20,000 + connector | 1 | Third-party iPaaS ($25K-$150K setup) |

Odoo is cheaper on paper, but keeping QB requires a third-party connector. NetSuite's connector costs ($25K-$150K) make it impractical for SMBs. Everything else is either more expensive, more fragmented, or both.
