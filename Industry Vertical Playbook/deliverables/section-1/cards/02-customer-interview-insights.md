## Appendix: Detailed Customer Evidence

### Theme 1: Customization Is the Primary Value Driver

- **Brown Safe:** 177 custom screens. 12-week manufacturing cycle tracked in a custom Production app.
- **Europena:** CRM + reporting + audits + opportunities + custom rolling data app. Considered switching — customization investment kept them.
- **Duraline:** Custom apps for Equipment, Commission, Purchase History, Email Status. Method is their system of record.
- **Mobility City:** 159 custom screens. Compliance tracking, standardized POS, rental management.
- **Vintage Makers:** Works with Yaser to build apps, buttons, and simplified flows.

**Sales implication:** Once 50+ custom screens, switching costs are real. Lead with "what would you build?"

### Theme 2: QuickBooks Attachment Is Permanent

- **Duraline:** Leadership prefers QB. AP managed there. Reports run from QB.
- **Brown Safe:** Heavy QBDT user. Resistant to QBO — "limited features."
- **Vintage Makers:** Inventory lives in QB Desktop Enterprise. NetSuite "too heavy and expensive."
- **Mobility City:** All franchise locations on QBO Plus.
- **Europena:** QBDT user. QB was one of the main reasons for choosing Method.

### Theme 3: Hybrid Businesses

- **Duraline:** Distributor + in-house product line + repair + refurbishment.
- **Vintage Makers:** Wine cellars + wholesale cigar distribution (3,400 SKUs, 6 states) + field installation.
- **Mobility City:** Equipment sales + repairs + rentals + franchise standardization (54 locations).
- **Europena:** Food distribution transitioning into food manufacturing. Two mirrored companies.

### Theme 4: Mobile Access

- **Vintage Makers:** Field reps on tablets — customer history, pricing, invoices on the spot.
- **Europena:** iPads for store-level audits with photos and notes. Web to Lead at trade shows.
- **Duraline:** Field crew with full work order context across locations.

### Theme 5: Cross-Department Adoption

- **Duraline:** Sales, shipping, customer care, AP, AR — 15 active users.
- **Mobility City:** 16 users Boca Raton, 10 Springfield, 7 Milpitas.

### Theme 6: DEP Revenue

- **Duraline:** Active DEP since Nov 2024. Custom rate ~$688/month (standard: $325/month).
- **Mobility City:** Dedicated consulting since August 2022.
- **Europena:** Heavy customization investment — CRM, reporting, audits, rolling data app.

### Theme 7: Known Friction Points

- Email limits: Duraline suspended at ~3K contacts, batches at 800.
- Performance: Heavy customization causes slowdowns.
- Reporting: Most reports run in QB — Method reporting less intuitive.
- Franchise compliance: Mobility City at 66%.
- QB sync: Occasional manual re-runs needed.
- Third-party inventory: Europena's Fishbowl data issues — switching to Markov.
- Too many steps: Europena PO → sales order → invoice → inventory is too many handoffs.
